# STS AI Lab

SynthThinkingSystems (STS) AI Lab is the research and engineering laboratory for STS.

Core Principles:

- Research first.
- Build second.
- Deploy third.

Current local AI stack:

- Ollama
- Llama 3.2 1B
- Python
- Modular AI Engine

The AI Lab records experiments, supports persistent memory, and is being designed as a reusable AI engineering platform.
