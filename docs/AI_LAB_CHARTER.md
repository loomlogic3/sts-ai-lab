# STS AI Lab Charter

## Mission

STS AI Lab is the research and engineering laboratory for SynthThinkingSystems.

Its mission is to study, test, document, and build practical AI systems that can later be integrated into STS products.

## Core Areas

- Local AI
- Large Language Models
- AI Agents
- RAG systems
- Voice AI
- Computer Vision
- AI evaluation
- AI infrastructure
- Product AI integration

## Rule

Research first. Build second. Deploy third.
